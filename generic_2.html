Travel Information

Transportation

Getting to San Miguel De Allende

The easiest way to reach San Miguel De Allende from the U.S. is by flying into either Querétaro (QRO) or León (BJX) airports. Both airports are about 1.5 hours from San Miguel by car. From there, you can arrange a shuttle, taxi, or rental car to reach the city. Direct flights to these airports are available from several major U.S. cities, making your journey smooth and straightforward.

Taxi Services To/From San Miguel

For private convenient transportation to, from, or around San Miguel De Allende, we recommend GERMAN CARDONA MENDIETA of Tu Transporte SMA. You and your guests can arrange taxis by contacting them via email at tutransportesma@gmail.com or WhatsApp at +52 415 103 5467. If you want to schedule a ride during your stay, simply send a WhatsApp message to this number for prompt service. Uber is also an option for an immediate ride. For shuttles to and from the airports, one option is BajioGO ( www.bajiogo.com/en ) which offers ride for $30 USD one way from either airport.
